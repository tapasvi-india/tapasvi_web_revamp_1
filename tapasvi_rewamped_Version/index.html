About Us

TAPASVI (Talented Aspirants Peoples' Action to the Society for Vigorous India) is a voluntary organization founded in 1994 and officially registered in 1997. It aims to promote human welfare, social justice, and community empowerment.

With a focus on the underprivileged, unemployed, and youth, TAPASVI works toward building a self-reliant society through skill development, leadership training, and social outreach. It collaborates with like-minded organizations to extend its impact across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and beyond.

Our Beginning

TAPASVI addresses community needs through surveys, awareness drives, and collaborative programs, focusing on social action, policy advocacy, and sustainable development.

Key Highlights:
- Health camps in slums
- Care centres for elders & children
- Skill training for women
- Support for the underprivileged
- Planned free health services

Our Mission

To achieve Peace, Unity and Brotherhood through community participation in spreading social awareness and ensuring economic development.

Our Vision

To work towards building an enlightened and humane society.

Our Goal

To reach more people from the underprivileged sectors and ensure a platform of hope, support, and help through our service.

Our Wings

Women Empowerment

“I measure the progress of a community by the degree of progress which women have achieved.” – B. R. Ambedkar

Tapasvi believes that empowered women can make meaningful contributions and live with dignity and respect.

Children's Welfare

“Children are the world’s most valuable resource and its best hope for the future.” – J. F. Kennedy

We provide food, clothing, shelter, and modern education to orphaned, challenged, and economically backward children.

Elders' Care

“Old age, believe me, is a good and pleasant thing.” – Confucius

We care for poor, abandoned senior citizens who need assistance and dignity in their golden years.

Goshala

We provide shelter for old, unproductive, and abandoned cows in traditional gaushalas. Cow seva is a sacred act of service in India.

CSR Collaborations

We have partnered with Infosys, Pinetrunk Enterprises and SBI to implement impactful projects, especially under our women empowerment initiatives.

Conservation

We promote sustainability through environmental protection, social equality, and economic development aligned with the UN’s SDGs.
